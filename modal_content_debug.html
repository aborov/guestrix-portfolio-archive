Extra spacious

Guests love this homes spaciousness for a comfortable stay.
Escape to your beautiful townhome in Gatlinburg, just minutes from downtown!

The townhome features 2 bedrooms (one with a King bed and the other with 2 Queen beds), and 2 bathrooms. Enjoy a full eat-in kitchen, a dining room, and a living room with fireplace. Relax on the balcony with pool views or unwind with 3 TVs throughout. Includes an assigned parking space for your convenience. Perfect for a memorable getaway!

High speed WIFI! Additional Parking available offsite upon request!

The space
Townhome with assigned parking space!! The entire 2 bedroom 2 bath condo is all yours!! No sharing!!

- Full Kitchen
- Dining room table
- Large Living room with walk out balcony
- Indoor Fireplace
- Primary Bedroom with King bed and ensuite bathroom
- Second bedroom has 2 Queen beds
- WIFI and TV
- Close to downtown Gatlinburg!
- Pool in the summer time!

Guest access
Our townhome is all yours alone to enjoy! You will given a keyless lock code so you can come and go anytime! No office to check in - just arrive after a long drive and use your personal keycode to let yourself into your private condo!

Other things to note
There is only one dedicated parking spot at the condo complex. If you have more than one car contact the host about additional parking.
