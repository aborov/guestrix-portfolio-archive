What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your browser when you visit a website. They help us remember your preferences, improve website performance, and understand user behavior.

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies – Required for the website to function properly (e.g., login sessions, authentication).
  • Performance Cookies – Help us analyze how users interact with our site to improve performance and user experience.
  • Functionality Cookies – Remember user preferences to provide a customized experience.
  • Analytics Cookies – Used by third-party services like Google Analytics to collect data about website traffic and user behavior.

Managing Cookies

You can set your browser to refuse cookies or alert you when cookies are being sent. However, some parts of our website may not function properly without cookies. Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings preferences.

Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party services (e.g., Google Analytics) that also place cookies to collect data about website traffic and user behavior. These third parties have their own privacy policies and cookie practices.
