Privacy Policy

Last updated August 2, 2025

At Guestrix.ai, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how Guestrix LLC ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and safeguards your data when you use our website and services.

By using our services, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

a. Host Information

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Property address and location (including GPS coordinates)
  • Publicly available property information, including but not limited to URLs, descriptions, photos, amenities, house rules, safety information, check-in and check-out instructions
  • Uploaded files and documents (e.g., guest guides, house rules, check-in instructions)

b. Guest Information

  • Messages and interactions with Staycee, our AI assistant
  • Metadata related to the session (e.g., timestamps, property linked, device type)

Note: Guests do not need to create an account to use Staycee, but their conversation transcripts are stored for quality assurance, service improvement, and to support host-guest interactions.

c. Technical Information

  • Browser type
  • Device identifiers
  • IP address
  • Pages visited
  • Session duration and activity

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Deliver and improve our AI concierge service
  • Personalize guest interactions based on property-specific content
  • Support host requests and troubleshooting
  • Analyze usage trends and improve system performance
  • Train and refine AI responses using anonymized data

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. Your data may be shared only:

  • With service providers (e.g., hosting or payment platforms) strictly for service delivery
  • To comply with legal obligations or law enforcement requests
  • To protect the rights, safety, or property of Guestrix LLC, our users, or the public

4. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary to:

  • Provide services to you
  • Comply with legal or contractual obligations
  • Improve system performance and reliability

Conversation transcripts are stored securely and used for internal analysis and AI model enhancement. Hosts may request the deletion of specific transcripts upon verification.

5. Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, no internet-based service is 100% secure, and we encourage you to use strong passwords and protect your devices.

6. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access or update your personal data
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Object to certain types of data processing
  • Export your data in a machine-readable format

To make a request, email us at hello@guestrix.ai.

7. Children's Privacy

Guestrix.ai is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. International Users

Our services are primarily intended for use in the United States. If you access Guestrix.ai from outside the U.S., please be aware that your data will be processed and stored in the United States, and subject to U.S. laws.
