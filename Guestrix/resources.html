Compare Host Payout by Platform

Why is Airbnb changing its fee structure? They want to make it easier for guests to see just one price. To keep the same payout, hosts using a PMS system must increase their prices. Our calculator shows you the appropriate price increase to achieve the same payout.

Assumptions based on Airbnb announcement and known fees:
• Airbnb (Pre-Oct 27, 2025): Standard fees: 3% host fee, ~14% guest fee.
• Airbnb (Post-Oct 27, 2025): Mandatory for PMS hosts, now a 15.5% host-only fee according to the newly announced fee change.
• Airbnb: No change for non-PMS hosts announced at the moment, but it is expected in December.
• VRBO: Pay-per-booking model with an 8% effective host fee (5% commission + 3% processing).
• Booking.com: Average commission is 15%.
