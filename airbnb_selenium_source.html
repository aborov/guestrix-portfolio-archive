Welcome to Bucktown! This bright, two-floor modern home has 3 bedrooms, 2.5 bathrooms, an open space living room, full dining room, full kitchen with s/s appliances, exercise equipment, and a large private patio w/ a fire pit.

Master suite features exposed brick, natural light, and a deck w/ comfy seating. Our home sits in the middle of a quiet, safe block w/ smart locks and thermostats keep things convenient, and you’ll love being walking distance from the train.

We can't wait to host you!
